FAQ

Answers to common questions from creators and sponsors.

Can I switch plans mid-season?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade at any time with pro-rated billing.

Do sponsors get listener insights?

Sponsors receive anonymized reporting on reach, retention, and conversions.

Is there support for production?

Studio Creator plans include editing, sound design, and recording support.

Do you help with sponsor matching?

Yes. We pair sponsors with shows based on audience fit and values alignment.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Annual agreements include a 10% savings and a dedicated onboarding timeline.

Can sponsors approve hosts before launch?

Yes. Sponsors review host shortlists and approve placements before the campaign begins.

What assets are included in the studio plans?

Studio tiers include editing, sound design, and a branded episode template pack.

Is there a trial period?

We offer a 30-day pilot with flexible upgrades after the first release cycle.
