Our Story

From a basement studio to a global network.

EchoGrid began with a single microphone and a simple idea: that everyone has a story worth telling. Our founders, former radio producers and audio engineers, saw a gap in the podcasting world—a lack of support for independent creators who wanted to produce studio-quality work without losing their unique voice.

Today, we represent over 500 shows across 50 categories. We've built a platform that not only distributes content but fosters a community of listeners and creators who believe in the power of audio to bridge divides and spark imagination.
